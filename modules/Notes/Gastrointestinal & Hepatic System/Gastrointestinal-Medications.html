Gastrointestinal Medications

Gastrointestinal & Hepatic System · Drug Reference

1. Antacids & Acid Reducers

🧪 Antacids

Calcium carbonate, Magnesium hydroxide

  • Use: Heartburn, indigestion, GERD
  • SE: Constipation (Ca-based) · Diarrhea (Mg-based)
💙 H2 Blockers

Ranitidine, Famotidine

  • Use: GERD, peptic ulcers
  • SE: Headache · Rare confusion in elderly
🛡️ Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs)

Omeprazole, Pantoprazole, Esomeprazole

  • Use: GERD, ulcers, GI bleed prevention
  • SE: Fracture risk · C. difficile · ↓ B12
🧬 Misoprostol

Misoprostol

  • Use: Ulcer prevention with NSAIDs
  • SE: Diarrhea · Cramping · Uterine contractions
⚠️ Pregnancy warning — causes uterine contractions

2. Laxatives & Stool Softeners

🌊 Osmotic

Polyethylene glycol (MiraLax), Lactulose

  • Use: Constipation; Lactulose also for hepatic encephalopathy (↓ ammonia)
  • SE: Diarrhea · Bloating
🌱 Bulk-Forming

Psyllium (Metamucil)

  • Use: Chronic constipation
  • SE: Bloating · Must take with plenty of fluids
🧴 Stool Softeners

Docusate sodium (Colace)

  • Use: Postpartum, post-op constipation prevention
  • SE: Mild cramping
💨 Stimulant

Bisacodyl, Senna

  • Use: Short-term constipation
  • SE: Cramping · Dependency with long-term use

3. Antiemetics

🎯 Serotonin Antagonist

Ondansetron (Zofran)

  • Use: Chemo-induced nausea, post-op nausea
  • SE: Constipation · QT prolongation
💤 Phenothiazines

Promethazine, Prochlorperazine

  • Use: Motion sickness, nausea
  • SE: Sedation · EPS (extrapyramidal) symptoms
🎢 Prokinetic

Metoclopramide (Reglan)

  • Use: Gastroparesis, reflux-related nausea
  • SE: Restlessness · EPS · Drowsiness

4. Antidiarrheals & Gut Modifiers

🚫 Opioid-Like

Loperamide (Imodium)

  • Use: Chronic diarrhea
  • SE: Constipation · Drowsiness
🔥 Bismuth

Bismuth subsalicylate (Pepto-Bismol)

  • Use: Upset stomach, diarrhea
  • SE: Black tongue/stools · Salicylate toxicity
⚠️ Probiotics

Lactobacillus

  • Use: Restore gut flora after antibiotics
  • SE: Gas · Bloating
💉 Bile Sequestrant

Cholestyramine

  • Use: Bile-acid diarrhea (e.g., Crohn's disease)
  • SE: Constipation · Binds other medications — take separately

5. GI Bleed, Liver & Pancreas

🧴 Cytoprotective

Sucralfate

  • Use: Ulcer protection — coats stomach lining
  • SE: Constipation · Take on empty stomach
💨 Osmotic Laxative / Hepatic

Lactulose

  • Use: Hepatic encephalopathy — ↓ ammonia absorption
  • SE: Diarrhea · Gas · Dehydration
🧬 Pancreatic Enzyme

Pancrelipase

  • Use: Pancreatic enzyme replacement (e.g., cystic fibrosis, chronic pancreatitis)
  • SE: GI upset if misused · Take WITH meals
💉 Somatostatin Analog

Octreotide

  • Use: Bleeding esophageal varices · Secretory diarrhea
  • SE: Bradycardia · GI upset

6. H. Pylori Treatment

🦠 Triple Therapy (Combo Antibiotics)

Amoxicillin + Clarithromycin + PPI (e.g., Omeprazole)

  • Use: Eradicate H. pylori infection → heals peptic ulcers
  • SE: GI upset · Must complete FULL course to prevent resistance
NCLEX: If patient asks if they can stop early because feeling better — No, must finish full antibiotic course.
