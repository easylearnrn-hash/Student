Rapid-Fire Disease Summaries

🐔 Chickenpox (Varicella)
  • Airborne + contact; VZV virus
  • All-stage lesions simultaneously (pathognomonic)
  • NO aspirin → Reye's syndrome
  • Acyclovir for high-risk; varicella vaccine (live)
  • Isolate until ALL lesions crusted
🎯 Lyme Disease
  • Black-legged tick ≥36–48 hr attachment
  • Bull's-eye rash (erythema migrans)
  • ELISA → Western Blot confirmation
  • Doxycycline; NOT for pregnancy/kids <8 (Amoxicillin)
  • Late: IV Ceftriaxone; pacemaker for carditis
☣️ Anthrax
  • NOT person-to-person
  • Cutaneous: black eschar; Inhalation: deadliest (bioterrorism)
  • Ciprofloxacin/Doxycycline; PEP = 60 days + 3 vaccine doses
  • Report to public health IMMEDIATELY
💩 C. difficile
  • Contact precautions; soap & water ONLY
  • After antibiotics (clindamycin, fluoroquinolones)
  • Oral Vancomycin first-line
  • NO antidiarrheals → toxic megacolon
  • Bleach for surfaces
🟡 Hepatitis
  • A & E = fecal-oral; B, C, D = blood
  • Jaundice + dark urine + clay stools = icteric phase
  • Monitor PT/INR (clotting factors)
  • Hep C: no vaccine; leading liver transplant cause
  • Hep D: only with HBV → HBV vaccine prevents D
😮 Mumps
  • Droplet; 5 days after parotid swelling
  • Parotitis = hallmark
  • Orchitis → infertility in post-pubertal males
  • MMR vaccine (live)
  • Treatment: supportive only
🌸 Rubella (3-Day Measles)
  • Droplet; 7 days after rash
  • Triad: fever + rash + postauricular nodes
  • CRS: cataracts + deafness + heart defects (1st trimester)
  • Check rubella titer at prenatal visit
  • MMR vaccine: avoid pregnancy 28 days post-vaccine
🔴 Rubeola (Measles)
  • Airborne; N95 + negative pressure room
  • 3 Cs prodrome: Cough + Coryza + Conjunctivitis
  • Koplik spots = pathognomonic
  • Vitamin A supplementation
  • Rash: head → toe, maculopapular
🧠 Meningitis
  • Triad: headache + stiff neck + fever
  • Kernig's (knee) + Brudzinski's (neck)
  • CT scan FIRST → then LP
  • IV antibiotics IMMEDIATELY — never wait for LP
  • Droplet 24 hr after starting antibiotics
🔴 HIV / AIDS
  • AIDS = CD4 <200 or OI
  • ELISA → Western Blot
  • ART lifelong (no skipping)
  • PCP prophylaxis (Bactrim) when CD4 <200
  • PEP within 72 hr; Standard precautions only
🫁 SARS / COVID-19
  • Airborne + contact; N95 + neg-pressure room
  • PCR = best early test
  • No SARS antiviral; COVID: remdesivir, dexamethasone
  • Main complication: ARDS
  • Prone positioning for ARDS

PPE Donning & Doffing — NCLEX Order

DONNING (Put ON):
  1. Hand hygiene
  2. Gown
  3. Mask / N95
  4. Goggles / Face shield
  5. Gloves
DOFFING (Remove — most → least contaminated):
  1. Gloves
  2. Goggles / Face shield
  3. Gown
  4. Mask / N95 (last)
  5. Hand hygiene

NCLEX Mnemonics & High-Yield Pearls

✈️ Airborne = "My TB Chicken Sars"
  • Measles (Rubeola)
  • Tuberculosis
  • Chickenpox (Varicella)
  • SARS / COVID-19
💧 Droplet = "Spiderman"
  • Scarlet fever, SARS (some)
  • Pertussis (whooping cough)
  • Influenza
  • Diphtheria
  • Epiglottitis
  • Rubella, Rubeola, RSV
  • Mumps, Meningitis (bacterial)
  • ANthrax (inhalation) — rarely droplet
🔑 Pathognomonic Signs
  • Koplik spots → Measles (Rubeola)
  • Bull's-eye rash → Lyme Disease
  • Parotitis → Mumps
  • Black eschar → Cutaneous Anthrax
  • All-stage lesions simultaneously → Chickenpox
  • Petechiae/purpura rash → Meningococcal meningitis
💊 Drug Pairing Must-Knows
  • Chickenpox: NO aspirin (Reye's) → use acetaminophen
  • C. diff: soap & water ONLY (no alcohol gel)
  • Lyme: Doxycycline (NOT if pregnant / child <8)
  • Meningitis: antibiotics NOW — don't wait for LP
  • HIV: PEP within 72 hr; CD4 <200 → Bactrim for PCP
  • Measles: Vitamin A supplementation
  • C. diff: Oral Vancomycin — NO antidiarrheals
🎯 Top NCLEX Test-Taking Rules for Infections:
  • Identify transmission route → choose correct precaution level
  • "What mask?" — Airborne = N95; Droplet = surgical mask
  • "What room?" — Airborne = negative pressure (door closed); Droplet = private room (door may open)
  • C. diff = bleach + soap; NO alcohol gel kills spores
  • Doff gloves FIRST, mask LAST
