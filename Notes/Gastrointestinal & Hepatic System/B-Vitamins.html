High-Yield Deficiencies

B1 — Thiamine
  • Beriberi: weight loss, emotional disturbances, impaired sensory perception, weakness & pain in limbs
  • Wernicke's encephalopathy: confusion, ataxia (poor coordination), ophthalmoplegia → give IV thiamine BEFORE glucose (glucose can precipitate/worsen Wernicke's)
  • Korsakoff syndrome: confabulation (making up memories), long-term memory loss — chronic phase
In suspected Wernicke's: IV thiamine first, then glucose — reversing this order can trigger/worsen brain damage.
B3 — Niacin (Pellagra)
  • 4 Ds: Diarrhea · Dermatitis (sun-exposed skin) · Dementia · Death (if untreated)
  • Seen in corn-heavy diets (low niacin/tryptophan)
B9 — Folate
  • Megaloblastic anemia: large, immature RBCs (hypersegmented neutrophils)
  • Neural tube defects in fetus → spina bifida, anencephaly
  • All women of childbearing age: 400–800 mcg/day folic acid supplement
Folate deficiency looks like B12 deficiency on CBC — differentiate by checking methylmalonic acid (elevated in B12 deficiency only).
B12 — Cobalamin
  • Requires intrinsic factor (secreted by gastric parietal cells) for absorption in terminal ileum
  • Pernicious anemia = autoimmune destruction of parietal cells → no intrinsic factor → B12 malabsorption
  • Neuro symptoms: peripheral neuropathy, subacute combined degeneration of spinal cord, gait disturbance
  • At risk: vegans, elderly, patients on long-term PPIs/metformin, post-gastrectomy patients
B12 deficiency + neuro symptoms = neurologic damage may be irreversible if not treated. Always treat before folate alone.

⚡ NCLEX Priorities

  • B1 (Thiamine): IV thiamine BEFORE glucose in alcoholics/malnourished
  • B3 (Niacin): 4 Ds = Pellagra (Diarrhea, Dermatitis, Dementia, Death)
  • B6 (Pyridoxine): Given with INH (isoniazid) to prevent peripheral neuropathy
  • B9 (Folate): Prevent neural tube defects — supplement before and during pregnancy
  • B12 (Cobalamin): Requires intrinsic factor; pernicious anemia = autoimmune; neuro symptoms may be irreversible
  • Vegans at risk for B12 deficiency (no animal products)
